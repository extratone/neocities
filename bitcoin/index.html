First Sighted Bitcoin

October 25 at Seattle, Washington
It finally happened!!! Somebody actually had a Bitcoin laying around when I asked them to show me one!!!
and when I exclaim FINALLY, I mean... I have been surrounded by relentless enthusiasm for the Big Moment of this thing... you have been panting and falling out of your chair talking about cryptocurrency right in front of me....
...for TWELVE YEARS. I am a 27-year-old man and I have been listening to you go on about this since I was in EIGHTH GRADE. I don't wear distressed Hollister cargo shorts anymore, so for the love of god... ASCEND to whatever rubik's cube ass playplace you've been talking about... or SHUT UP.
whatever I'm looking at right now... I'm pretty sure I'd rather just carry cash around than one of these!!!
((some perspective: the same amount of time has passed that did between the end of the Korean War and the beginning of the Vietnam war.))
